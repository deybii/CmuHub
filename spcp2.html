PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology major in Multimedia Arts (BS IT) is a four-year degree program that prepares students with knowledge and skills in multimedia design and development. The program covers methodological and systematic knowledge on the acquisition, deployment, and management of information technology resources and services. As a Multimedia Arts major, it prepares students necessary skills in delivering rich creative experiences across various media. By specializing in the field of multimedia arts, students will obtain a foundation in 3D Graphics, animation, print, web interactive, and mobile technology. It allows students to gain conceptual, technical, aesthetic, and professionals skill for innovation storytelling on all media forms. The course is focused on the production of a powerful creative environment and the expansion of studentsâ€™ creative capacity to deliver across media platforms. The program will enhance the mastery of user interaction, 2D and 3D design, video editing, mobile application, and other digital experience from prototype to final output.
