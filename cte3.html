Programs Offered

  • Bachelor in Elementary Education (BEED)
  • Bachelor in Secondary Education (BSE)
  • Bachelor of Early Childhood Education (BECED)

PROGRAM OVERVIEW

The Bachelor of Early Childhood Education (BS CEd) is a four-year degree program that equips students with the fundamental understanding and application of the principles of early childhood care and education, as well as experience in the application of these principles.

This program aims to educate individuals to be competent early childhood practitioners working with children from 0-8 years old in various early childhood settings (home, community, school, and workplace.)

CAREERS AND PROFESSIONS

  • Preschool Teacher
  • Kindergarten and Elementary School Teacher
  • Nanny
  • Childcare Center
  • Childcare Worker
  • Special Education Teacher
  • School Counselor or Psychologist
