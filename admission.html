Admission

Application for CMU Undergraduate Admission is ongoing!

The City of Malabon University is committed to maintaining high academic and intellectual standards and promoting diversity and inclusivity in its student body. To achieve these goals, the university has developed a well-defined Admission Policy that sets clear and objective criteria for student selection. To get more details about the Admission Policy, Guidelines and Procedures, please access the CMU Undergraduate Admission Primer through this link: https://bit.ly/cmu-admission-primer

Undergraduate Admission Procedure:
ALL INCOMING FIRST YEAR COLLEGE students who intend to enroll in CMU must complete the Undergraduate Admission Procedure.
STEP 1: Submit the Accomplished Admission Forms and requirements at the Registrar's Office
Application Form: https://bit.ly/cmu-ua-application-form
Reccomendation Form: https://bit.ly/cmu-ua-recommendation-form
Step 2: Pay the Admission Fee of Php 300.00 at the Finance Office
Step 3: Take the CMU Undergraduate Admission Test
Step 4: Pass the Admission Interview
Step 5: Submit the Medical Examination Certificate/Clearance issued by any DOH-accredited medical facilities
Step 6: Enroll onsite during the scheduled enrollment period.
Admission Application Period: April 12 to May 12, 2023

For inquiries, you may e-mail us at u.admissions@cityofmalabonuniversity.edu.ph or visit the Office of the University Registrar at the 3rd floor of the CMU Administration Bldg. Thank you.
