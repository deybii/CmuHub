CMU Performing Arts Groups Usher in the Festive Season with Dazzling Performance at Malabon's Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony

_December 3, 2023

The CMU Chorale and CMU Golden Blues participated in the Christmas Tree Lighting Ceremony held on December 1, 2023, at Welcome Park Letre, Tonsuya. Led by our City Mayor, Hon. Jeannie N. Sandavol, the ceremony witnessed the presence of City of Malabon officials and personnel. The highlight of the evening was the lighting of the 40ft Christmas tree, adorned with vibrant lanterns, Christmas balls, reindeers, and Santa Claus.
