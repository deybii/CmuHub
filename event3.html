CMU-School of Graduate Studies Seminar:
Introduction to SPSS

_November 26, 2023

The CMU School of Graduate Studies initiated the Seminar on SPSS Utilization for Research Statistics on November 25, 2023, at the Audio-Visual Center. Dr. Irene Dalog from De La Salle Araneta University facilitated the event, providing invaluable insights that significantly deepened students’ comprehension of SPSS’s crucial role in research statistics. The seminar was graced by Dr. Glen De Leon, CPA, University President, and Dr. Willam Enrique, VP for Academic Affairs and SGS OIC Dean. Notably, SGS Professors Dr. Allie Agustin and Dr. Janine Dela Cruz were also present in the training. As we carry forth the knowledge gained, let this seminar serve as a beacon guiding us in the intricate realm of statistical research. For further details kindly refer to the link: https://tinyurl.com/Introduction-to-SPSS
